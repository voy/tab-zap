You finish reading an article and the tab just… stays open. You look something up, open three more tabs, forget about them. You're researching a trip and suddenly you have seventeen tabs about hotels in Lisbon that you're definitely not going back to.

Tab Zap is a Chrome extension that helps you clean up. Click the icon and it groups your open tabs — all your Gmail tabs, all your Twitter tabs, everything from the same site, and the ones you haven't touched in days. One click closes the whole group.

Need to be more selective? Open any group to see a checklist and uncheck the tabs you want to keep before closing.

Prefer the keyboard? Tab Zap is fully keyboard-driven: navigate groups with j/k, open with l, close with d, go back with h. Press ? to see all shortcuts.

No setup, no folders, no fuss. Your browser is not a to-do list — Tab Zap helps you treat it like one less thing to worry about.
