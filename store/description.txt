Tab Zap is a tab manager carefully crafted for a minimalist aesthetic and keyboard-first workflow — built exactly the way I want to use it myself. No clutter, no settings rabbit holes, just a clean way to close the tabs you no longer need.

Click the icon and it shows you what to close — other tabs on the same site as the one you're on, and the biggest clusters across your open tabs. One click closes the whole group.

Need to be more selective? Open any group to see a checklist and uncheck the tabs you want to keep before closing.

Fully keyboard-driven: navigate with arrow keys, or vim-style bindings if you're that way inclined. Press ? to see all shortcuts.

No setup, no folders, no fuss. Your browser is not a to-do list — Tab Zap helps you treat it like one less thing to worry about.
